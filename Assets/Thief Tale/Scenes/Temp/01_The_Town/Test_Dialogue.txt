The Dialogue_System script will print line by line.
Putting a number sign will end the first section of dialogue.
#<---This skips the line and ends the current dialogue.
The repeat line will restart the dialogue at that line number.
Names, and protraits are self explanitory. 
Checking the toggle "Right" will put the portrait on the right side.
If you put in a camera it will cut to that cam until another cam is added.
Added cameras need to have thier "Depth" set to 1.
It will auto return to the game play cam when dialogue finishes.
